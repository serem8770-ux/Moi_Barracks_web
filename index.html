Our Story

Founded in 1960, Moi Barracks High School was established to provide quality secondary education to children of Kenya Defence Forces personnel and the broader Eldoret community. Over six decades, we have grown into one of Uasin Gishu County's most distinguished institutions.

Our school motto, "Hard Work Crowns", is not just a phrase — it is a way of life that every student, teacher, and staff member lives by. We combine rigorous academics with military discipline, environmental stewardship, and character-building activities.

How to Apply

01

Obtain Application Form

Download from this website or pick up at the school office. Forms are available free of charge during working hours.

02

Submit Documents

Attach KCPE result slip (Form 1), previous school report cards, birth certificate, and parent/guardian ID copy.

03

Selection & Placement

The school uses KCPE results and county placement. Top performers are given priority. Medical certificates required.

04

Receive Admission Letter

Successful candidates receive an official admission letter. Pay fees and report on the designated opening day.
