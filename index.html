Our Story

Founded in 1960, Moi Barracks School was established to provide quality education to children of Kenya Defence Forces personnel and the surrounding community. Over six decades, we have grown into one of the region's most distinguished institutions.

Our approach combines rigorous academic training with the discipline and values instilled by our military heritage — producing graduates who excel in universities, the armed forces, and professional fields across Kenya and beyond.

How to Apply

01

Obtain Application Form

Download from this website, pick up at the school office, or request via email. Forms are free of charge.

02

Submit Documents

Attach birth certificate, previous school report cards, transfer certificate (Form 1), parent/guardian ID copy.

03

Entrance Assessment

All applicants sit a short assessment in Mathematics and English. Dates communicated upon receiving application.

04

Admission Letter

Successful candidates receive an official admission letter within 2 weeks. Pay fees and report on opening day.
