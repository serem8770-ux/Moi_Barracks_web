Our Story

Founded in 1960, Moi Barracks Senior School was established to provide quality senior secondary education to children of Kenya Defence Forces personnel and the broader Eldoret community. Over six decades, we have grown into one of Uasin Gishu County's most distinguished senior secondary institutions.

We proudly offer both the 844 curriculum (KCSE) and the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC), fully conforming to Kenya's current education system and ensuring every learner finds their rightful pathway to excellence.

Our motto, "Hard Work Crowns", is not just a phrase — it is a way of life that every learner, teacher, and staff member lives by, combining rigorous academics with military discipline, environmental stewardship, and character development.

Applications for Form 1 (844) and Grade 10 (CBC) for the 2027 academic year are now open.

How to Apply

01

Obtain Application Form

Pick up at the school office during working hours. Forms are available free of charge for both the 844 and CBC pathways.

02

Submit Documents

Form 1 (844): KCPE result slip, previous school report cards, birth certificate, parent/guardian ID.
Grade 10 (CBC): Junior Secondary completion certificate, competency reports.

03

Selection & Placement

Selection is based on academic performance and county placement. Medical certificates are required for all new learners.

04

Receive Admission Letter

Successful learners receive an official admission letter. Pay fees and report on the designated opening day.
